Hungarian Chicken Paprikash

Chicken Paprikash just like the Hungarians like it

Need quick dinner ideas?

You will whip this one in minutes


Here's the list of ingredients

  • Egg Noodles
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 tablespoon of chopped parsley
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 3 shallots, finely chopped

Here are the steps to create the perfect Hungarian Chicken Paprikash

  1. Fill a large pot with salted water
  2. In the instant pot, turn on the saut√® function and add oil, shallots, and garlic
  3. Season chicken with 1 teaspoon of salt and half a teaspoon of pepper
  4. On the instant pot, select timer for 12 minutes
  5. Release pressure naturally
